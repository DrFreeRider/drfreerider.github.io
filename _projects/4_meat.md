Using a discrete choice experiment in the Netherlands, we estimate willingness to support policy bundles aimed at reducing meat consumption and production, examining how preferences vary with individual values, contextual framing, and policy design.

**Co-authors:** Joana Wensing & Daniel Polman

**Stage:** Analysis
