This field experiment evaluates whether management innovation information reduces food waste in small and medium-sized restaurants in Cusco, Peru. We examine adoption rates, behavioral mechanisms, and heterogeneous effects across firm types.

**Co-authors:** Cesar del Pozo & Francisco Alpizar

**Stage:** Under review
