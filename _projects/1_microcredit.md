We study whether complementary business training improves business performance and loan repayment among microfinance borrowers in Colombia, and whether repayment behavior is mediated by borrowers' feelings of reciprocity toward the lending institution.

**Co-authors:** Burak Urak (Williams College) & Francesco Cecchi (Wageningen University)

**Stage:** Writing

**Pre-registration:** [AEA Registry](https://www.socialscienceregistry.org/trials/15977/history/262667)
