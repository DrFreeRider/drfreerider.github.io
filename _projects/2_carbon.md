We evaluate the impact of an emerging carbon credit program on coffee farmers in Tanzania, where smallholders receive payments for carbon fixed by trees on their plots. Using quasi-experimental methods to assess short-run effects and a randomized design for long-run evaluation, we examine impacts on farm management, livelihoods, and environmental outcomes.

**Co-authors:** Erwin Bulte (Wageningen University) & Gerald Lesseri (Wageningen University)

**Stage:** Analysis
